1. To combine parts: 
    /combine("child_part","parent_part","plug_name","socket_name")
    eg: 
    /combine("Hub_Cover_Output","Casing_Top","plug_main","socket_hub_output")
    /combine("Hub_Cover_Input","Casing_Top","plug_main","socket_hub_input")
    /combine("Hub_Cover_Small","Casing_Top","plug_main","socket_hub_small")
    /combine("M6_Hub_Bolt","Casing_Top","plug_main","socket_bolt_hub_1")

2. To separate combined parts: 
    /separate("combined_part_name")
    eg: 
    /separate("Casing_Top")

3. To move parts to the working ares:
    /focus("part_name")
    eg:
    /focus("Casing_Base")

4. Assembly sequence using shortcut functions:
    /combine_casing_top
    /combine_casing_base
    /combine("Casing_Top","Casing_Base","plug_casing_mate","socket_casing_mate")
    /combine_casing_bolt
